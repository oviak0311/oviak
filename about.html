ABOUT

Welcome to Bruins Dance! A club where we believe in self-expression and creativity through dance.

We invite everyone of all levels to explore the world of dance in a positive environment.
Our club offers many styles of dance as well as amazing workshops and performances hosted by choreographers.
We aim to help individuals grow and connect with one another and express their creativity in the world of dance!
